Who We Are

Engineering solutions built on
38 Years Experience.

We support industries across the globe to build reliable, safe and code-compliant process equipment — from concept design through fabrication, testing and site commissioning.

SISCO Energy Private Limited — formerly Acoustics India Private Limited — has been engineering and manufacturing high-quality industrial equipment since 1986. With six integrated ISO certifications, AS9100 Rev D aerospace approval and ASME U Stamp, we supply Oil & Gas, Refinery, Defence, Aerospace and Nuclear sectors worldwide.
